Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Arkiary ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Diary Entries: Text content you write in your journal entries, stored locally on your device.
  • Mood Data: Mood selections you make when creating entries.
  • Chat Messages: Conversations with the AI companion, processed via OpenAI's API.
  • Voice Data: If you use voice chat, audio is processed in real-time via OpenAI's Realtime API and is not stored by us.
  • Time Capsules: Content you create for future viewing.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes.
  • Usage Data: Basic app interaction data to improve user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core features (journaling, AI chat, mood tracking)
  • To process AI-powered responses through OpenAI's services
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To process in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit

Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All diary entries, mood data, and time capsules are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • AI Processing: Chat messages and voice data are sent to OpenAI's servers for processing only after you provide explicit consent within the app. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices. OpenAI does not use API data to train models.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not upload or sync your personal data to any cloud servers.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

  • OpenAI (openai.com): Powers AI chat and voice features. When you use AI chat, the text of your messages is sent to OpenAI for processing and generating responses. When you use voice chat, your audio is processed via OpenAI's Realtime API and is not stored after processing. The app requests your explicit consent before sending any data to OpenAI for the first time. OpenAI processes data according to its API usage policies and does not use API data to train its models.
  • Apple StoreKit: Handles in-app purchases and subscriptions. Managed entirely by Apple.
  • We do not share your data with any third party other than OpenAI as described above.

Your Rights

  • All your data is stored locally — you can delete it at any time by deleting the app.
  • You can stop using AI features at any time without affecting your journal data.

Children's Privacy

Arkiary is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: arkiary.app@gmail.com
